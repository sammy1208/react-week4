The late morning light shone a bright white through the library’s large windows, illuminating his notes scrawled messily across parchment. He was doing a final round of studying on Itachiyama’s nobility in preparation for the arrival of their royal family. He’d been kept protected from outsider alphas his whole life, chaperoned constantly and never allowed to even entertain the thought of a future with anyone.
